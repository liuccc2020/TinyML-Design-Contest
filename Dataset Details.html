Dataset Details
Dataset
 The organizers have prepared a large dataset for IEGMs from implantable devices.

80% of the data will be released to the contestants.

20% of the data will be reserved for evaluation.

Description
 The dataset contains 2-15 minutes IEGM recordings over 95 subjects with 250 Hz sampling rate, 16-bit resolution and 15-55 Hz bandpass filter.
