Frequently Asked Questions
Q1: How many people form a team?
A1:
 For each team, the number of team members is not restricted.
Q2: Can we add/remove members and/or advisors?
A2:
 No. You are not allowed to add/remove members and/or advisors after successful registeration.
Q3: How can we send our solver to you? via Email?
A3:
 We will set up a google form . All submissions should be done via google form. For the detailed requirement, please click here.
Q4: Could we cite a paper that is accepted but not published yet?
A4:
 The contestants can refer to a paper that is accepted but not published yet. Typically, the contestants need not to submit any document to describe their algorithm. The contestants only need to submit their program code.
Q5: Are the monetary awards only for the students?
A5:
 Yes. The recipients of monetary award must be students.
Q6: What is Preliminary submission?
A6:
 Before your final program submission, we provide a preliminary test for your programs. The preliminary test help you verify the correctness and performances of your programs which will be executed in our evaluation environment. We will provide you the evaluation results of each test. Based on them, you can further improve your programs before the final program submission.
Q7: If we do not submit files in the preliminary test, are we still qualified to submit files for the final submission?
A7:
 Yes. Even you do not submit files in the preliminary test, you can still submit files in the final tests. The preliminary tests are only for your own references. The contest awards will be given based on the evaluation of your final submissions.
