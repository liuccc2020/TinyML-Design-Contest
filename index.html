Introduction

2022 ACM/IEEE TinyML Design Contest Collocated with ICCAD is a challenging, multi-month, research and development competition, focusing on advanced, real-world problems in the field of Electronic Design Automation (EDA). It is open to multi-person teams world-wide. The prizes will be awarded at an ICCAD special session dedicated to this contest.

Tentative Schedule
Contest release date July 15, 2022
Registration deadline July 30, 2022
Submission due September 30, 2022

 October 15, 2022
Invited talks and winners announced At ICCAD Conference (October 30 - November 3, 2022)
